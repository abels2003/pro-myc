PROMYC® AS AN INDUSTRIAL INGREDIENT

Promyc® is a vegan mycoprotein ingredient produced through biotechnology via the fermentation process as a core. Our production process allows us to be a lot more efficient than traditional farming in terms of time, cost, and feedstock, with much less environmental impact. It also enables the decentralization of food production, making it possible to produce Promyc® wherever needed.

PROMYC

Promyc® is in fact quite a boring ingredient — neutral colour, taste, and smell. But boring doesn’t necessarily mean a bad thing. Besides being excellent in color, taste, and smell, Promyc® is a sustainable mycoprotein ingredient that can be used to produce vegan food products and that brings a great source of fiber and protein, nutrition, and healthy vitamins while offering a great texture.

High Nutritional value

“No compromise” means a tasty experience without hurting you, the environment, or anything else really. Great meals come with proper balanced nutrition, which helps you get full of energy.
